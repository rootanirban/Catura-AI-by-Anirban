About

Built by one person,
for everyone

Catura AI started as a personal project to make AI genuinely useful for everyday tasks — without subscriptions, without complexity.

Mission

Make AI accessible

Catura AI was created with one clear goal: give everyone access to a smart, reliable AI assistant — completely free. Most AI tools lock their best features behind expensive plans. We don't. Catura AI is free, fast, and built to be useful from the first message.

Technology

What powers it

Catura AI is built on modern language model infrastructure with a custom interface designed for speed and clarity. It uses secure authentication via Supabase, bot protection via Cloudflare Turnstile, and a lightweight frontend with zero unnecessary dependencies.

Privacy

Your data, kept simple

We collect only what is needed to run your account. No selling of data, no tracking across the web, no third-party ad networks. Your conversations are yours. We're transparent about what we store and why.

The Creator

Founder & Developer

Hello,
I'm Anirban Das. I'm a 21-year-old BCA student from India, and Catura AI is a project I've been building since 2025. The idea started with a simple question: what if I could build the AI assistant I always wanted to use? I was fascinated by artificial intelligence and dreamed of creating something that wasn't just powerful, but genuinely useful in everyday life. Building Catura AI hasn't been easy. I've faced countless challenges—from backend issues and API integrations to frontend bugs and endless debugging sessions. Every feature, improvement, and update has been built and maintained by me, often through long nights of learning, testing, and refining. What makes Catura AI different is its philosophy. It's completely free to use, with no credit card requirements, no hidden fees, and no unnecessary barriers. I believe advanced AI tools should be available to everyone, especially people who can't afford expensive subscriptions. My goal isn't to create another complicated AI platform. I want Catura AI to feel simple, approachable, and reliable. When someone uses it, I want them to feel like they're talking to a helpful friend—always available, always ready to assist, and focused on providing clear, direct answers. Catura AI is not backed by investors or a large company. It's an independent project built with curiosity, passion, and a commitment to making AI more accessible for everyone. Thank you for being part of the journey.
